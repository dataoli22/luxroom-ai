🏠 Finding student housing in Luxembourg nearly broke me. So I built something to help the next person do it faster.

When I arrived as an international student, the hunt was brutal: refreshing a dozen housing sites every few hours (good rooms vanish in *hours*), competing with everyone else, and writing enquiry emails to landlords in French and German — languages I don't speak.

So I built **LuxRoom AI** — a free, open-source desktop app that handles the tedious parts for you:

🔎 Watches Luxembourg housing sites around the clock and pings you the moment a match appears — even while you sleep
⭐ Scores every listing against your budget, commute and preferences, so you only look at the good ones
✉️ Drafts a polite outreach message *in the landlord's own language* (FR / DE / LB / EN), ready for you to review and send
🔒 Runs entirely on your own laptop — free, no subscriptions, nothing leaves your device

I want to be honest about what it is: an **assistant**, not magic. A big part of the market lives in Facebook/WhatsApp groups and university housing that no app can reach — so it ships with a complete **Student Housing Guide** (in 🇬🇧 English, 🇫🇷 French and 🇩🇪 German) covering those groups, scam warnings, deposits, domiciliation, and a week-by-week plan.

It's **open source and free forever** — built for students, not for profit. If it saves you even a few days of stress during your search, that's the whole point. And if you'd like to make it better — fix a source, add a site, report a bug, or translate the guide — contributions of any size are genuinely welcome (you don't have to be a programmer). 💛

👉 GitHub: https://github.com/dataoli22/luxroom-ai

Huge thanks to everyone who tested it along the way. Sharing this with @University of Luxembourg and every incoming student — please pass it on to anyone about to start their housing search. 🙏
